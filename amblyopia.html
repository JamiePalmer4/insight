Amblyopia

Symptomns of Amblyopia: One Eye has Poor Vision.

Other Symptomns Include:

  • Squinting the Eye
  • Closing One Eye
  • Tilting the individuals head

Treatment for Amblyopia: Wearing an Eye Patch or using Eye Drops.
