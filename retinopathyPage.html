Diabetic Retinopathy

Symptomns found Early On: No Symptomns.

Symptomns found Later On Include:

  • Blurry Vision
  • Spots Floating in the Eye Vision
  • Blindness

Treatment for Diabetic Retinopathy: Surgery, Laser Treatment, or Injections.
